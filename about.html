Why we built a concierge service at the crossroads of two worlds.

Who We Are

BlackBookBroker is a premium concierge service operating across Gibraltar and Southern Spain. We're based on the ground, living and working on both sides of the border, with deep local connections built over years in the community.

We started because we saw a gap. Gibraltar attracts high-net-worth visitors, relocating professionals, and international businesses, but there was nobody offering a single, trusted point of contact who could handle everything from restaurant reservations to relocation paperwork. No app, no call centre. Just a real person who picks up the phone and gets things done.

We open doors that don't have handles. That's not a tagline. It's what we do, every day, for every client.

Why Gibraltar

Gibraltar sits at one of the most unique crossroads in Europe. A British Overseas Territory connected to Spain, home to a thriving financial services sector, a growing gaming industry, and a constant flow of international visitors drawn to the Rock.

With the upcoming Schengen agreement set to remove the physical border, the region is about to change dramatically. More people, more businesses, more demand for someone who understands how both sides work. We're positioning ourselves now to be the name people trust when that shift happens.
